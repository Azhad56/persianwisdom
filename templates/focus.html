FOCUS AND SCOPE

The Persian Wisdom is an international, peer-reviewed, open-access e-journal published on a quarterly basis. The Journal focuses on studies in Persianate societies, culture and history and aims to publish research articles that explore the topic from a broad array of Persianate world. The editors particularly encourage submissions of research papers that discuss and touch upon any areas that have direct or indirect link to the Persian sources. The journal aims to reach a wide international audience and encourages contributors from different fields of higher education to submit work for publication. The editors also welcome suggestions for special issues on particular topics as well as contributions in the form of book reviews.

The journal is multidisciplinary and bilingual and invites scholars from relevant disciplines including Persian, history, linguistics, sociology, and culture studies and all areas of educational research having primary sources in Persian language. The Persian Wisdom quarterly journal welcomes papers from all prospective authors.
